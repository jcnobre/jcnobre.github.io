I am an Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic School of the University of Vale do Rio dos Sinos (UNISINOS), where I teach courses on computer networks. I am also a postdoctoral researcher at the Research Group on Computer Networks and Multimedia Communication of the Federal University of Pará (UFPA), Brazil. I am Electrical Engineer (UFRGS - 2003) and I hold a M.Sc. (UFRGS - 2010) and Ph.D. (UFRGS - 2015) in Computer Science.

During my doctoral studies, I performed a one-year internship (ended in August 2012) at Cisco Systems (San Jose, USA). There I performed research activities as a member of the Network Operating Systems Technology Group (NOSTG) under the topic of Network Management.

In this page you will find information about my current activities, the research projects I'm involved with, and recent publications.
